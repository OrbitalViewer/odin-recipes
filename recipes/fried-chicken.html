Crispy Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

This classic fried chicken recipe delivers a crispy, golden-brown crust with juicy, flavorful meat inside. Marinated in seasoned buttermilk and coated in a spiced flour blend, it’s perfect for satisfying comfort food cravings. Serve it hot with your favorite sides or chill it for a picnic treat.

Ingredients

Marinade:

  • 1 kg chicken pieces (bone-in, skin-on preferred)
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika

Flour Coating:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)

For Frying:

  • Vegetable oil (enough for deep frying)

Fried Chicken - Step-by-Step

  1. Marinate the chicken:

    • In a large bowl, mix buttermilk with salt, garlic powder, pepper, and paprika.
    • Add the chicken pieces and coat thoroughly.
    • Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

  2. Prepare the flour coating:

    • In a separate bowl, mix all flour coating ingredients until evenly combined.

  3. Coat the chicken:

    • Remove chicken from the marinade, letting excess drip off.
    • Dredge each piece in the flour mixture, pressing to coat thoroughly.
    • Place coated pieces on a wire rack and let rest 10-15 minutes for better adhesion.

  4. Fry the chicken:

    • Heat oil in a large pot or deep fryer to 175°C (350°F).
    • Fry 3-4 pieces at a time for 10-14 minutes, depending on size, until golden brown and cooked through.
    • Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate or wire rack to drain excess oil.

  5. Serve:

    • Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce or classic sides.
    • Optionally chill and pack for later — fried chicken is great cold too.
